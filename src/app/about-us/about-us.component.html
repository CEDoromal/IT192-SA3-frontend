About Us

Orange is an e-commerce website prioritizing the security, safety, and convenience of our customers, ensuring them that all the sellers in our platform are legitimate.

We have been operating since the year 2021 and have served over 9 billion people in total. Through utter diligence, determination, and honesty of everyone in our community, the number of customers that we serve daily continue to grow.


Our Mission

Orange is committed on bringing our customers high quality products that are both genuine and affordable without the need for them to leave their house.

Our Vision

Through our platform, the anxiety of buying things online will become a thing of the past. No more fake products. No more scams.
