About Us

PawDoption is a city veterinary office that caters to the needs of dogs but also serves as a temporary home for dogs.

Committed to helping find loving homes for these lovable dogs and giving them the family they need.

Dogs that are open for adoption are thoroughly screened for both the human and the dog's safety.


Our Mission

PawDoption is committed on promoting animal health and welfare, supporting adopt, not shop campaign.

Our Vision

A healthy number of human-dog relationships and an increased number of adopted dogs throughout the city.
